How it works
Create and sell NFTs
Set up your wallet Once you’ve set up your wallet of choice, connect it to Anicraft by clicking the NFT Marketplace in the top right corner. Learn about the wallets we support.
Create your collections Click Create and set up your collection. Add social links, a description, profile & banner images, and set a secondary sales fee..
